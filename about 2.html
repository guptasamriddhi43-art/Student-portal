srmcem lucknow Shri Ramswaroop Memorial College of Engineering and Management (SRMCEM), Lucknow: An overview Shri Ramswaroop Memorial College of Engineering and Management (SRMCEM), in Lucknow, Uttar Pradesh, is a private institute established in 1999. It is affiliated with Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam Technical University (AKTU) and holds approvals from UGC, AICTE, and NBA accreditation. SRMCEM offers various undergraduate, postgraduate, and diploma programs in engineering, management, and other professional fields. Courses offered
Undergraduate: B.Tech (various specializations including Computer Science & Engineering, AI & Machine Learning, Information Technology, etc.), B.Pharm, BBA, BCA, B.Com (Hons). Postgraduate: M.Tech (Computer Science & Engineering, Civil Engineering, Environmental Engineering), MBA, MCA. Diploma: D.Pharm. Admission process Admission is primarily based on entrance exams and UPTAC counselling. Specific entrance exams include JEE Main for B.Tech, GATE for M.Tech, JEECUP for D.Pharm, and CUET UG/PG for most other UG/PG courses. Lateral entry is available for some programs, and there are reserved seats for management quota and fee waivers for economically weaker candidates.
Fees & eligibility The eligibility criteria generally involve completing 10+2 or a bachelor's degree with a minimum percentage, in addition to relevant entrance exam scores. Total fees vary by program, for example: B.Tech: ₹4.94 Lakhs - ₹6.26 Lakhs. MBA: ₹2.42 Lakhs. MCA: ₹2.37 Lakhs.
Placements SRMCEM has a placement cell that facilitates student recruitment. In 2023, the highest package was INR 48 LPA and the average was INR 4 LPA. Recruiters include Accenture, Cognizant, and Deloitte. Facilities The campus provides smart classrooms, labs, a library, and Wi-Fi. Separate hostels for boys and girls include amenities like mess facilities, sports, a gym, and medical services. Additional facilities include shuttle services, seminar halls, and a canteen.
Ranking India Today ranked SRMCEM #62 out of 238 for B.Tech in India in 2022. Shiksha rates SRMCEM 3.5 out of 5 based on student reviews. Important dates for 2025 Key dates for 2025 include the UPTAC Counselling Last Date Of Registration on June 30 and Round 1 Choice Filling in the first week of August. Tentative exam dates for CAT and MAT are November 30, September 21 (PBT), and September 28 (CBT) respectively.
